PUMILI KA KAPATID, PARA SA BAYAN


ON WAR ON DRUGS:
We will support the war on drugs in a manner that is within the bounds of the law. Yung na-achieve ng administrasyong Duterte, talagang lahat ng maaring gawin sa enforcement ay ginawa nila. Kailangan din natin tignan yung prevention, na tinuturaan natin 'yung mga bata na huwag kayong papasok diyan sa mga klaseng ginagawa. At 'yung mga nasabit na o 'yung mga naadik na, may maganda tayong mga rehab center para matulungan natin sila. Basically there has been a misunderstanding about what we are actually doing. The review is actually wanting to know the accountability that we have and so that is exactly what we want to present...They are open to comments, they are open to present their own criticisms, opinions, however we will continue to pursue our own line of action. Kailangan litisin ‘yung mga pusher ... withe the due process of law. Kung ito’y talagang magiging cancer na ng society, diyan pwedeng patawan ng malaking parusa Si President at kapulisan natin ay ayaw nila lumabag sa batas. Sinusunod nila ang due process… Alam n'yo 'yung iba kasi, may ibang mga drug lord pinapatay na rin nila 'yung mga tao nila dahil baka magsumbong. 'Yun ang nangyayari,
Sen. Panfilo Lacson noted how President Rodrigo Duterte admitted how his administration’s banner programs of anti-illegal drugs and fighting corruption, have “fallen short even of his expectations.” Are we going to resort to extreme measures like tokhang? Extrajudicial killing? Hindi naman po kailangan. If we have God in our needs, masosolve natin 'yan... Nahuhuli 'yung drug lord, wala e, pinapatakas. Ang pinapatay 'yung maliliit, 'yung ating mamamayan. At the onset of the administration's 'war on drugs' I and the organizations to which I belong have registered our condemnation. and continue to do so. Drug addiction must be treated as a complex disease and must therefore be treated scientifically through rehabilitation. You know, there was a golden opportunity that happened during the early days of this so-called drug war. I’m not saying that it’s a good approach…it was a horrible approach that you start killing people in the communities. Very clearly, based on official data, despite the killings of Filipinos and all the money spent, the amount of shabu and drug money we’ve seized has not gone beyond 1 percent of those in circulation.
